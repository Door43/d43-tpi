===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Libyans and the Ethiopians** - names of the people who live in Libya, which is west of Egypt, and in Ethiopia, which is south of Egypt; Libya, Egypt, and Ethiopia are in northeast Africa, (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
