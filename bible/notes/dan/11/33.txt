===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **will give understanding to many** - "will help many people understand" 
  * **they will be taken as prisoners and robbed of their possessions** - AT: "their enemies will put them in prison and steal their possessions" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **receive a little help** - this report implies that they did not get much help; using an implied contrast between little and much
  * **they may be refined, cleansed, and made pure** - AT: "that God may refine them, cleanse them, and make them pure" or "that God may make them more holy people" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **time of the end** - "end of time" or "when time is no more"
