===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Instead of them** - "them" refers to the gods mentioned in [[en:bible:notes:dan:11:36|11:37]]
  * **honor the god of fortresses** - AT: 1) "defend his fortresses" (UDB), 2) "attack strong fortresses," or 3) "build strong fortresses"
  * **divide up the land for a price** - AT: "the king will sell land to his followers"
