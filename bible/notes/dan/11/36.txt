===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The king will do** - the king of the North
  * **exalt and magnify** - The words "exalt" (lift up) and "magnify" (make large) are used together for emphasis (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
  * **speak astonishing things** - AT: "say terrifying things"
  * **the God of gods** - AT: "the one true God above all other false gods" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **until the wrath is completed** - AT: "until he finishes hurting people in his rage and anger" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
