===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Many will be purified, made pure, and be refined** - AT: "God will purify, make pure, and refine many people" These three terms mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **the wicked will act wickedly** - AT: "the wicked people will act wickedly" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **refined** - to be purified by removing anything that doesn't belong
  * **the regular burnt offering is ... is set up** - AT: “the king of the North takes away the regular burnt offering and sets up the abomination that makes devastation” (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **1,290 days** - (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
