===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **King Nebuchadnezzar sent** - AT: "Nebuchadnezzar sent his messengers with" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **all peoples, nations, and languages** - "all peoples of every nation and language"
  * **May your peace increase** - This is a common greeting.
  * **It seemed good to me** - The word "me" here refers to Nebuchadnezzar.
  * **signs and wonders** - Both of these words have the same meaning. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **How great are his signs, and how mighty are his wonders!** - This is a doublet; both of these phrases have the same meaning.
  * **His kingdom is ... generation to generation** - This is a doublet; both of these phrases have the same meaning.
