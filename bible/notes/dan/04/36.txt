===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I was brought back to my throne, and even more greatness was given to me.** - AT: “I returned to rule my kingdom again, and I received even more greatness.” (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) and (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **He can humble those who walk in their own pride** - AT: "He can humble those who are too proud." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **praise, extol, and honor** - All three of these words have basically the same meaning. AT: "praise and honor" (UDB); (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
