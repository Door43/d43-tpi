===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **This decree against Nebuchadnezzar was carried out immediately** - AT: “This decree against Nebuchadnezzar happened immediately.” (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **He was driven away from people** - AT: "His advisers sent him away from the other people." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])

There are three similes in this verse. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])

  * **He ate grass like an ox** - AT: "He ate grass like cows do."
  * **His hair grew as long as eagles' feathers** - AT: "His hair was as long as eagles' feathers."
  * **his nails became like birds' claws** - AT: "His fingernails looked like birds' claws."
