  * **the third year of Cyrus king** - "year 3 of the rule of Cyrus the king." (See: Ordinal Numbers)
  * **a word was revealed to Daniel** - AT: "God revealed a word to Daniel" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive|Active or Passive]])
  * **who was also called Belteshazzar** - AT: "whose name was also Belteshazzar"
  * **having understanding from the vision** - AT: “when he gained understanding from the vision”
