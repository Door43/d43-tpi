===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **city gates **- sometimes "city gates" are used to represent the entire city/town. Ancient towns had walls all around and gates to enter or exit the town, keeping the place protected from enemies.
  * **with all the desire of his soul - **  This term means that the Levite had a strong inward desire to serve.
  * **will chose as his sanctuary**  - Yahweh would in the future choose Jerusalem as this chosen place.
  * **who stand there before Yahweh -**  "who stand to minister before Yahweh" or "who stand to serve before Yahweh"
