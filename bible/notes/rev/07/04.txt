===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **who were sealed**- AT: "who had the the mark of God upon them" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **twelve thousand**- 12, 000 in each tribe (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **144,000**  - These are the twelve groups of 12,000 from each tribe. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **every tribe of the people of Israel **- There were twelve tribes in Israel, each named for one of Jacob's sons.
