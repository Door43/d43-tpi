===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **your spokesmen** - "your prophets and priests who speak for me and for you"
  * **I will hand Jacob over to the destructive ban, and Israel to abusive humiliation** - Here "Jacob" and "Israel" mean the same thing. AT: "I will allow the enemy to completely destroy and humiliate the people of Israel" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
