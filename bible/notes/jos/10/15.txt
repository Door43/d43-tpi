===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **five kings**  - the number of kings being referenced (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **hidden**  - AT: "kept out of sight"; "unseen"
    * **Makkedah** - called “a place of shepherds”.  A chief city of the Canaanites.  Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:10, 21, 28, 29, 12:16 and 15:41. (See:[[en:obe:other:shepherd]], [[:tpi:obe:other:canaan]] and [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
