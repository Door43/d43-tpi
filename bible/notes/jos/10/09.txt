===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **Joshua came**  - A figure of speech referring to the entire army of Israel by the name of the commander, Joshua. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Beth Horon**  - Means "house of the hollow" or "of the cavern". The name of two towns or villages. Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:11, 16:3, 5, 18:13, 14, 21:22, 1 Samuel 13:18, 1 Kings 9:17, 1 Chronicles 6:68, 7:24, 2 Chronicles 8:5 and 25:13. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Azekah**  - Name of a place or location. Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:11, 15:35, 1 Samuel 17:1, 2 Chronicles 11:9, Nehemiah 11:30 and Jeremiah 34:7.
    * **Makkedah**  - Name of a place or location. Translate the same way as in Joshua 10:16, 17, 21, 28, 29, 12:16 and 15:41. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
