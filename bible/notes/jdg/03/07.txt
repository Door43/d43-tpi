===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **They worshiped**  - The people of Israel worshiped
    * **the anger of Yahweh was kindled**  - Yahweh's anger is being compared to a fire being lit. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **sold them into the hand of Cushan Rishathaim** - Allowing the people of Israel to be conquered is compared to being sold. AT: "allowed them to be conquered by Cushan Rishathaim" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
    * **Cushan Rishathaim**  - This is the name of a man. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **Aram Naharaim**  - This is the name of a country. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
