===== Translation Notes: =====
Gideon replies to the men from Ephraim.

    * **Abi Ezer** - This is the name of a man.  He was Gideon's ancestor and Gideon refers to him to refer to his family and their land. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]], [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
    * **victory over the princes of Midian—Oreb and Zeeb**  - Gideon is using the princes of these regions to refer to the armies of these regions that the princes led into battle. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Oreb and Zeeb** - See how these names were translated in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:07:24|7:25]].
    * **What have I done now compared to you?**  - Gideon uses this question to honor the people of Ephraim and express they have done more great things than he has.  (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
    * **died down**  - "became less"
