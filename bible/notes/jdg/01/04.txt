===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **They killed ten thousand** - "They killed 10,000" AT: "They killed many" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **of them** - AT: "soldiers of the Canaanites and Perizzites" or "enemies"
    * **Bezek** - An area in the mountains of Canaan. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **They found Adoni Bezek** - AT: "They found Adoni Bezek and his army" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Adoni Bezek** - This is the name of a man, he was the leader of the army of the Canaanites and the Perizzites. The word "Adoni" means "lord of" or "my lord."
