===== Translation Notes: =====
Deborah and Barak's song continues using poetry. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])

    * **Hear, you kings! Pay attention, you leaders** - Both of these phrases mean the same thing.  It is repeated for emphasis. 
    * **you went out from Seir, when you marched from the region of Edom** - Seir is a mountain on the border of the land of Israel and Edom.  Both of these phrases refer to Yahweh bringing the people of Israel into the land of Israel.
    * **the earth shook, and the heavens also trembled**  - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis.
