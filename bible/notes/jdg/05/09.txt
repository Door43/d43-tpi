===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **My heart goes out to commanders of Israel** - The word "heart" refers to emotions. AT: "I appreciate the commanders of Israel" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **you who ride on white donkeys...you who walk along the road** - This describes the rich as those who ride donkeys and the poor as those who walk. AT: "Both those who are rich and those who are poor" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
