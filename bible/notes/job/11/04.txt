===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **my belifs are pure - This saying means"my understanding is correct"**
  * **I am blameless in your eyes. - This means " I am innocent before you."**
  * **open his lips- This expression means to speak. (See: Idiom) and (See:Doublet)**
  * **secrets of wisdom****- This expression means the way Zophar perceives truth as apposed to Job's understanding, coming from God.**
  * **demands less than you deserve** - This expression means, "is punishing you less than you deserve".
