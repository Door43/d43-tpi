===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Why then have you brought me out of the womb?**  - AT: "Why have you allowed me to be born?"
  * **I wish I had given up my spirit **- AT: "I wish that I had died" or "Would that I had succumbed"
  * that no eye had seen me - This expression means that he wishes that he died before or at birth.
