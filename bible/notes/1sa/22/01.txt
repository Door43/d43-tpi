===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **So David left there** - "So David left Gath"
    * **cave**  - a large hole in the side of a hill or under the ground
    * **Adullam**  - This is the name of a city. (See: Translate Names) 
    * **Everyone who was in distress, every one who was in debt, and every one who was discontented—they all gathered to him** - AT: "Many people who were distressed, owed debts, or were unhappy came to him" (See: Hyperbole)
    * **four hundred**  - "400" (See: Translate Numbers)
