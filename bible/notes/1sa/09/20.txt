===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **all the desires of Israel**  - This refers to the nation's need for a king like other nations. They hope Saul will be a great king to begin the royal line for Israel. (See: Implicit - Explicit)
  * **smallest of the tribes ……least of all the clans- Saul implies that he does not feel worthy. (See: Implicit - Explicit)**
