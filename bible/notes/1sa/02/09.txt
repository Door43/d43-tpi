===== Translation Notes: =====

  * guard the feet - This is a figure of speech. (See: Synedoche)
  * put to silence in darkness - This is a euphomism referring to the place of the unsaved dead.
  * **prevail- This means to defeat ones enemy, especially after a long fight.**
  * **prevail by strenght**- This means no one will be successful in life's struggles and problems by his own strength of wealth or ability.
