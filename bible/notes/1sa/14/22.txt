===== Translation Notes: =====

The Philistines started to fight against each other and the Israelites had begun fighting the Philistines.

    * **the battle passed beyond Beth Aven**  - "Beth Aven" is a place in Israel. The name means "house of wickedness" or "house of pride." See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:1sa:13:05|13:05]]. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
