===== Translation Notes: =====

Jonathan and his weapon carrier have gone to see what the Philistine soldiers are doing. 

    * ** staying on the outskirts of Gibeah** - "staying on the edge of Gibeah." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **Gibeah** - The word "Gibeah" or Gibah means "hill" and is a place in Israel. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **under the pomegranate tree** - The word "pomegranate" refers to a thick skinned fruit that is round and red and has many seeds to eat.
    * **that is in Migron** - "Migron" is a place in Israel. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
    * **six hundred men were with him** - "600 men were with him" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **the son of Ahitub, Ichabod's brother** - "Ahitub" means "the brother of goodness." (See:[[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]]) 
    * **Ichabod** - "no glory" or "the glory has departed."  See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:1sa:04:21|4:21]]. 
    * **Phinehas, the son of Eli**  - "Eli" means "lifted up." See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:1sa:01:03|1:03]].
