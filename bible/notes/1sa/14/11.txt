===== Translation Notes: =====

Jonathan and the man carrying his weapons decided to show themselves to the Philistines and see how the soldiers replied to them.

    * **coming out of the holes**  - The Israelites were not really hiding in holes or caves. The Philistine soldiers were trying to make them feel bad with scornful words because the Israelites were not boldly coming after their enemy.
