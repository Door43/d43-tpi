===== Translation Notes: =====

While Jonathan is with the people, he has just accused his father of keeping them from killing as many Philistines because of the great hunger of the people.

    * **Michmash**  - See how you translated the name of this town in [[en:bible:notes:1sa:13:01|13:02]].
    * **Aijalon**  - "Aijalon" is a place which means "deer field" and is in Zebulun in Israel. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
    * **people rushed greedily**  - Here the word "greedily" means the people ate without waiting for or without helping others.
    * **ate them with the blood** - Because of God's laws in the Old Testament, God required that his people drain the blood before eating an animal's meat.
