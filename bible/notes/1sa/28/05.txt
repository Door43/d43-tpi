===== anslation Notes: =====

  * **Philistines - The Philistines were a coastal people who lived east of the tribe of Judah and north of the tribe of Simeon, at the time of David. **
  * trembled in his heart - This means he was very afraid.(See: Idiom)
  * **Urim-The high priest carried Urim and Thummin in his breastplate, in a marked pouch, next to his heart. This was used to discern God's will in any important manner. In the UDB this is referred to as"throw the marked stones.**
  * **Endor- Endor was a nearby town.**
  * **talk with the dead- This woman claimed special powers, to be able to talk with people who had already died. These dead people were thought to be able to tell the future and give special wisdom.**
