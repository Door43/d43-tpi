===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **So Saul removed him from his presence**  - "So Saul removed David from his presence"
    * **a thousand**  - "one thousand" or "1,000"(See: Translate Numbers)
    * **David went out and came in before the people**  - David led the soldiers "out" to battle and led them "in" to safety, and the "people" of Israel respected David. (See: Explicit - Implicit)
