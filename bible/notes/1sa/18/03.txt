===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **made a covenant of friendship**  - AT: "promised to always be close friends"
    * **Jonathan loved him as his own soul**  - See how you translated this in 18:1.
    * **Jonathan took off the robe ... and belt**  - Jonathan did this to honor David and to make them similar in appearance. David may have still been wearing his shepherd's clothes. (See: Explicit - Implicit)
    * **belt**  - a piece of cloth or leather worn around the waist or across the chest
