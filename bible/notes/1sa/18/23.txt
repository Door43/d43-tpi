===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **spoke these words to David**  - AT: "said to David what Saul told them" (See: Metonymy)
    * **Is it a small matter ... and lightly esteemed?**  - David asks this question to express his belief that he is not worthy of the honor. AT: "It would be a great honor to become the king's son-in-law. But I do not think that I should do that, because I am only a poor and insignificant man." (UDB) (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **son-in-law** - See how you translated this in 18:18.
