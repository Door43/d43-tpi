===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Michal** - This is the name of Saul's youngest daughter. (See: Translate Names)
    * **Michal ... loved David** - Here "loved" means she had romantic feelings for him. 
    * **They told Saul** - "David and Michal told Saul"
    * **the hand of the Philistines may be against him**  - See how you translated this in 18:17. AT: "the Philistines will kill him" (See: Metaphor)
    * **son-in-law**  - See how you translated this in 18:18. AT: "daughter's husband"
