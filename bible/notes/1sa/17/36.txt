===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **a bear** - This is a large wild animal with teeth and claws to tear apart their prey.
    * **This uncircumcised Philistine**  - David intends this phrase as an insult.
    * **will be like one of them**  - David is saying that he will kill the Philistine.
    * **challenged the armies of the living God** - David is referring to the army of Israel.
