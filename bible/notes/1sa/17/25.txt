===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **"Have you seen this man who has come up?**  - The phrase "this man" is referring to Goliath the Philistine. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **He has come to challenge Israel.**  - This can also be explained as, Goliath has come to invite the people of Israel to fight him. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **And the king will give the man who kills him great riches**  - This can also be explained as, And the king will give the man who kills Goliath great riches (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **will give him his daughter in marriage**  - This can also be explained as, will give the man who kills Goliath the daughter of the king to marry. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **will make his father’s house**  - This can also be explained as the relatives of the man who kills Goliath.
