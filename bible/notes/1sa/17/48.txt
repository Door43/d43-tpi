===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **slung it**  - In verse 39, this phrase means to put a stone in a leather strap, hold both ends, twirl it around and let the stone fly through the air.
    * **The stone hit Goliath on the forehead**  - The forehead is the part of the skull just above the eyes.
