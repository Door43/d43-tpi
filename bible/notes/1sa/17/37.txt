===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **the paw of the lion and from the paw of the bear** - These animals have very strong front legs with claws on the paws they use to help capture prey.
    * **the hand of this Philistine** - This can be physical rescue.  It can also mean rescue from the power of this Philistine.  (See: [[:tpi:bible:team-info:training:quick-reference:figures:metaphor]])
    * **a helmet of bronze** - This is a head covering for display as well as protection. 
    * **clothed David with his armor** - This is a general term to describe all of the protective covering.  (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **a coat of chainmail** - This is the armor covering the body made out of linked metal rings.
