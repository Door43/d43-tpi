===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **bear**  - This is a large animal with thick hair and sharp, curved parts on the toes, that walks on 4 legs but can stand on two legs as a person does.
    * **flock**  - This is a group of the same type of animal.
    * **chased after him and attacked him, and rescued it out of his mouth.**  - This can also be translated as "chased after the bear or lion and attacked the bear or lion and rescued the lamb out of the bear or lion's mouth." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **And when he rose up against me, I caught him by his
beard, struck him, and killed him.**  - This can also be translated as, "and when the bear or lion rose up against me, I caught the bear or lion by the bear or lion's beard, hit the bear or lion and killed the bear or lion." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
    * **beard**  - This can also be explained as hair on the side of the face or chin.
