===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **passed over**  - "passed over the border between Israel and Philistia"
  * **six hundred men**  - 600 (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
  * **Achish...Maoch...Nabal**  - These are names of men (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Achish, the son of Maoch, king of Gath**  - Achish is the king, not Maoch.  Maoch is Achish's father.
  * **David lived with Achish at Gath**  - David and his men did not live in the same house or compound as Achish, they just lived in the same city.
  * **David lived**  - Here, "lived" is used in the sense of staying in the same place for a long time.
  * **Ahinoam...Abigail**  - These are names of women
  * **Saul was told**  - AT: "Someone told Saul" or "Saul heard" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
