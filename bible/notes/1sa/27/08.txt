===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **David and his men attacked various places**  - This was something that happened many times, not something that just happened once.
  * **Girzites** -  A people group who lived somewhere between Philistia and Egypt (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **who were there from ancient times**  - In the UDB, this reads "That area extended south from Telam."  The phrase could either be naming the place Telam or saying they were there from ancient times.
  * **Shur**  - region on the northeast border of Egypt.  See how you translated this in [[:tpi:bible:notes:1sa:15:06|15:7]].
  * **David attacked the land**  - AT: "David attacked the people in these lands" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]).
  * **David attacked**  - AT: "David and his men" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]]).
