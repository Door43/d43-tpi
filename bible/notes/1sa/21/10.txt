===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Achish**  - This is the name of a man who was a king. (See: Translate Names)
    * **Is not this David the king of the land?**  - The servants ask this question to say that they believe it is David. Here the word "king" means commander or king of the army. AT: "This is David, the great man of Israel." (See: Rhetorical Question and Ambiguity)
    * **Did they not sing to one another of him in dances ...?**  - A question is used to reveal information about David that had become well known. AT: "They sang to one another of him in dances ..." (See: Rhetorical Question)
    * **Saul has killed his thousands, And David his ten thousands**  - See how you translated this in 18:7.
