===== Translation Notes: =====


Obadiah has just told the people of Edom that they should not have watched Israel's ruin and that they should not have helped Israel's enemy.

  * **your deeds will return upon your own head** - AT: "what you did to Israel will happen to you" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **For as you have drunk upon my holy mountain** - This is a picture of how Esau had taken advantage of Israel's disaster. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **drunk upon my holy mountain** - The words "my holy mountain" refer to Jerusalem and is in contrast to the mountain of Esau ([[en:bible:notes:oba:01:07|01:8]])  
  * **so will all the nations drink continually**  - All the nations will persecute Israel, but they will be punished as God was going to punish Edom. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **They will drink, and swallow**  - The nations will continue to suffer, possibly rapidly, the punishment from God. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
