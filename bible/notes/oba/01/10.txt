===== Translation Notes: =====

Obadiah has just told the people of Edom that they will be tricked and their wise men will be killed. 

  * **you will be covered with shame **- "shame will cover you." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **cut off forever** -"destroyed forever"
  * **you stood aloof, on the day strangers carried away his wealth **-"you stood apart, on the day strangers carried away his wealth" AT: "You watched while strangers carried Israel's wealth away"
  * **aloof** - "apart" or "indifferent"
  * **foreigners entered his gates** - The word "foreigners" refers to "non-native people."
  * **cast lots upon Jerusalem** - The words "cast lots" mean "gambled" or "made bets."
