===== Translation Notes: =====

Obadiah has just said that some of Jacob's descendants will escape and that none Esau's descendants will escape in the day of Yahweh's wrath.

  * **Zarephath **- a city south of Sidon, a place on the coast of the Mediterranean. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Sepharad **- 1) Sparda in western Asia Minor 2) it means 'boundary' in Assyrian, 3) Sepharam in Northern Palestine.
  * **Saviors will go up**  - The word "Saviors" means "deliverers."
