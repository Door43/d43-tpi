===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues the account of Jesus praying in the garden of Gethsemane.

  * **While he was still speaking **- "While Jesus was still speaking"
  * **Now the betrayer of him had given them a sign, saying, "Whomever I kiss, he is the one. Seize him."**  - AT: "Now his betrayer had given them a sign, telling them to seize the one he kissed." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
  * **sign **- signal
  * **Whomever I kiss**  - "The one I will kiss" or "The man whom I will kiss" (UDB)
  * **kiss **- a respectful way to meet a friend
