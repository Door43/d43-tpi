===== Translation Notes: =====

Jesus continues to speak against the religious leaders because of their hypocrisy.

  * **fill up the quota of your fathers' sin**  - "you finish the sins your ancestors began" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy|Metonymy]])
  * **quota **-
  * **You serpents, you offspring of vipers**  - "you are as evil as dangerous and poisonous snakes" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor|Metaphor]])
  * **how will you escape the judgment of hell?**  - "there is no way for you to escape the judgment of hell!" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
