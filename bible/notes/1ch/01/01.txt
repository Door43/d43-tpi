===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **Adam, Seth**  - These are all names of people. Translate them how you would translate names in your language. See: How to Translate Names. 
    * **Adam, Seth, Enosh, Kenan, Mahalalel, Jared**  - These names are a list telling the ancestors of the people. If your language has a specific way to mark this kind of list, you can use it here.
    * **Noah, Shem, Ham, and Japheth**  - Noah is the father of three sons: Shem, Ham, and Japheth. Most likely Japheth is the oldest (first) son, Shem is the next (second) son, and Ham is the youngest (third) son.
