===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **this boasting ... will not be silenced...**  - Paul will continue to boast through out all of Achaia which is modern day southern Greece.  (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
  * **And why? Because I do not love you? **-  AT: "I will not stop boasting about serving you without being paid because it shows that God knows and others will know that I love you." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
