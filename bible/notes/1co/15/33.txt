===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Be not deceived** - "Do not believe a lie" (See: Active/Passive)
  * **"Bad company corrupts good morals."**  - AT: "Don't spend time with sinful people, or you will start being sinful."
  * **morals** - "habits of how to behave".
  * **Sober up!**  - AT: "Think seriously about this!"
  * **some of you have no knowledge of God** - AT: "some of you act as if you don't know God at all." (See: Hyperbole)
  * **I say this to your shame - **  AT: "You should be embarrassed by this."
