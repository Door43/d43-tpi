===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Beth Eked of the shepherd -** The name of a place where sheep were sheared.
  * **brothers of Ahaziah**  - "relatives of Ahaziah" (UDB)
  * **going down to greet**  - "going to visit"
  * **"Take them alive."**  - "Seize them!" (UDB) AT: "Capture them so we can kill them".  (Jehu did not want them "alive".)
  * **So they took them alive**  - "So they captured them"
  * **He did not leave any of them alive.**  - "He killed all of them."  (See: Euphemism)
