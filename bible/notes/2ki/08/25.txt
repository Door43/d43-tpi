===== Translation Notes: =====

Ahaziah becomes king of Judah.

  * **in the twelfth year of Joram**  - "in the twelfth year of the reign of Joram king of Israel"
  * **Athaliah, Omri**  - (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Ahaziah walked in the ways of**  - "Ahaziah lived the same way as"
  * **the house of Ahab**  - "the family of Ahab" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **evil in Yahweh's sight**  - "that Yahweh thought were evil" (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
