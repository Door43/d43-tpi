===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **special object that are to be brought into the house of Yahweh**  - "sacred offerings to buy things for the temple" (UDB)
  * **assessed**  - To assess means to set the amount that a person is required to pay; as in a tax. AT: "required to give"
  * **motivated by Yahweh in their hearts to give**
 - "inspired by Yahweh to give" or "directed by Yahweh to give" (See: Synecdoche) "that they themselves decide to give" (UDB)
  * **every one of them from the taxpapers**  - AT: "every priest must take the money from the people who pay it"
  * **maintain**  - "keep in good condition"
