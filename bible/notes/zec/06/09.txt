===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came to me**  - The prophet Zechariah received his vision from an angel of God who expressed God's message.
  * **an offering**  - "a gift," especially a gift given to God, his priests, or a royal kingdom.
  * **the exiles**  - "the Israelites who were captured and taken from their homes to Babylon"
