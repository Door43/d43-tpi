===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Discretion will watch over you, understanding will guard you.** - AT: "Because you know what is right and wrong you will not be making mistakes," ([[:tpi:bible:notes:pro:02:16|2:16]]; See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) also (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
  * **Discretion** - being careful in actions and speech
  * ** They will rescue you from the way of evil, from those who speak perverse things** - AT: "You will not do and say what evil people do and say." See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]]) also (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]).
  * **abandon** - leave and never return to someone or something
