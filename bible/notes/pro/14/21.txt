  * **contempt** - "disdain" or "scorn"
  * **Do they not go astray who plot evil?** - This question is an indirect way of saying that people who plan evil are on the wrong path, and moving away from God. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
