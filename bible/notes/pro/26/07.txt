===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **paralytic **- a person who is unable to move or feel all or part of his body.
  * **The legs of a paralytic hanging down are like a proverb in he mouth of fools **- AT: "Like the legs of a lame hang loose, so is a parable in the mouth of fools." (See: Simile; Metonymy)
  * **Tying a stone in a sling**  - as a stone bound to a sling; or as to put a precious stone among a heap of stones, the attempt of honoring the fool is a lost action. (See: Simile; Metaphor)
