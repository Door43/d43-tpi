===== Important Terms: =====

  * [[:tpi:obe:kt:wise|wise, wisdom]]

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **he has no strength to lift it up to his mouth **- this is a hyperbolic expression, sets off his extreme laziness.
  * **seven men with discernment **- AT: "seven men who can give a reason." Note that seven can be a round number of plurality. (See: Translate Numbers)
