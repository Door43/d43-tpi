====== Proverbs 26:18-19 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm> \v 18 Crazy people who shoot burning arrows to kill people \q1 \v 19 are as foolish as those who deceive someone else \q2 and then say, "I was only joking." \b \q1 </usfm>

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Like a madman who shoots burning arrows **- As one who makes himself mad by doing mischief. (See: Simile)
  * **"Was I not telling a joke****?**"- The madman is only playing, to show himself as being mad by exhibiting danger in public. (See: Rhetorical question)
