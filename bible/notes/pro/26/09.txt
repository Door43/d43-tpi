===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **a proverb in the mouth of fools **- clause of same structure is seen at 26:7. "A thorn in a drunkard's hand" means an action hurtful to himself and others. (See: Parallelism; Simile; Metonymy)
  * **hire**  - to use or receive the services of someone to do a particular job.
  * **the one who hires a fool or just anyone who passes by **- AT: "but the one who hires a fool is like he hires anyone that passes by." (See: Simile)
