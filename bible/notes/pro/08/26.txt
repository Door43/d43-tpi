===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **established** - brought into being on a stable basis
  * **drew the horizon on the face of the deep** - "created the curved line of the circle of the earth which is seen in the distance where the surface of the ocean meets the sky"
  * **drew the horizon** - the word in the Hebrew original language contains the idea of drawing a round circle
