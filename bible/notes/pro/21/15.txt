===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **but it brings terror to evildoers -**  this clause shows contrasts to the previous one with the use of "but", AT: "but it brings destruction to the workers of evil." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
  * **he will rest in the assembly of the dead**  - AT: "he will remain in the assembly of the dead spirits" or "he will remain in the assembly of the giants." (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]] and [[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
