===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **The violence of the wicked will sweep them away**  - AT: "The violence of the wicked will destroy and remove them completely."
  * **The way of a guilty person is crooked **- the mode of action of a guilty man is distorted and strange. (See: [[:tpi:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the one who is pure **- his or her work is right, just and regular.
