===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tomorrow **- **1)**  as the day following today or, **2)**  in future time.
  * **Let someone else praise you and not your own mouth; a stranger and not your own lips**  - both mean the same idea, that praise comes from the external (See: Doublet).
