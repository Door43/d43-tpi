===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **A fool’s lips bring**  - "what a fool says brings" (See:[[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **his mouth invites a beating** - AT: "his speech calls for someone to flog him"
    * **his mouth**  - "his speech" (See:[[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **invites** - calls for or deserves (See:[[:tpi:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **a beating**  - "a flogging" or "a whipping" This is probably in the sense of punishment rather than violence.
