===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **if he does something that is prohibited**  - Prohibited' means 'not allowed'. Other synonyms are 'banned' or 'forbidden' or not permitted''.
  * **for his sin which he has committed**  - 'Committed' here means 'to do a wrong act'.
