===== ULB: =====

<usfm>
\v 18 He will put some of the blood on the horns of the altar that is before Yahweh, which is in the tent of meeting, and he pour out all the blood at the base of the altar for burnt offerings, which is at the entrance of the tent of meeting.

===== Important Terms: =====

  * burnt offering

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **horns of the altar **- These 'horns' are where the corners project out or point out according to UDB.
  * **base of the altar**  - The 'base' of the altar is the bottom part that meets the floor or goes up from the ground.
  * **cut off all the fat from it and burn it **- The pronoun 'it' refers to the bull from the verse before.
