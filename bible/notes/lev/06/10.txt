===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **linen clothes**  - This is clothing made of flax thread.
  * **He will pick up th****e ashes **- "He will lift up the ashes" or "gather the ashes".
  * **ashes that are left**  - "The remaining ashes" or "the leftover ashes" (See: Active - Passive)
  * garments - clothing (not in any vocab lists)
