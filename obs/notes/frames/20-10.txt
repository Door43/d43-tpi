===== God Remembered His Promise [20-10] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-10.jpg?nolink&}}

God i bin givim hevi long ol manmeri bilong em long pasin nogut bilong ol na rausim ol i go long narapela kantri. Em i no lus tingting long ol na long promis bilong em. God i wok long lukluk long ol manmeri bilong em na em i toktok long ol long maus bilong ol profet. Em i promis olsem, bihain long 70 yia pinis, ol bai i kam bek gen long promis graun.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **exile** - See how you translated this term in **[[:tpi:obs:notes:frames:20-09|[20-09]]]**.
  * **did not forget** - This could be translated as, "did not ignore" or, "did not neglect." Or the whole phrase could be translated as, "continued to honor his commitment to his people and his promises."
  * **watch over** - That is, "take care of."
  * **speak to them through his prophets** - This could be translated as, "tell his prophets messages that he wanted them to tell his people."
