===== The Two Sons [35-03] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-03.jpg?nolink&}}

“Wanpela man i gat tupela pikinini man i stap. Liklik pikinini man i tokim papa bilong em olsem, 'Papa, mi laik yu givim mi samting bilong mi long bihain, nau mi laik yu givim mi!' Olsem na papa i brukim ol samting bilong em namel long tupela pikinini man bilong em.”

===== Important Terms: =====

  * **[[:tpi:obe:kt:son]]**
  * **[[:tpi:obe:kt:inherit]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus tells the story.)
  * **my inheritance** - This could be translated as, "the part of your wealth that will be rightfully mine when you die."
  * **property** - That is, "wealth" or, "possessions." This property would probably have included land, animals, and money.
