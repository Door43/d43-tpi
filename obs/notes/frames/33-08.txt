===== The Desire for Riches [33-08] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-08.jpg?nolink&}}

“Graun i gat nil gras long en em i olsem manmeri i harim tok bilong God, tasol taim i go on na ol kago na bilas na amamas bilong laip i pasin laikim bilong ol long God. Dispela as wanem samting Jisas i skulim ol i no karim gutpela kaikai. Dispela graun i gat planti gras nogut i pasim sid kaikai i no karim kaikai.”

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * (Jesus continues to explain the story.)
  * **thorny ground** - See how you translated this term in **[[:tpi:obs:notes:frames:33-04|[33-04]]]**.
  * **is a person** - See how you translated this comparison in **[[:tpi:obs:notes:frames:33-06|[33-06]]]**.
  * **cares** - That is, "concerns" or, "needs" or, "problems."
  * **riches** - That is, "desire for wealth."
  * **pleasures of life** - This could also be translated as, "doing things to get pleasure" or, "focusing on things that give pleasure."
  * **choke out** - That is, "squelch" or, "destroy" or, "overcome." This phrase could also be translated as, "cause him to stop loving God."
  * **does not produce fruit** - That is, "does not produce spiritual fruit" or "does not behave in a way that shows God's Spirit is working in him."
