===== God's Promise to David [17-07] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-07.jpg?nolink&}}

Tasol God i salim dispela tok wantaim profet Natan long Devit olsem, “Yu no inap wokim tempel bilong mi, long wanem yu man bilong pait. Pikinini man bilong yu bai wokim. Tasol, mi bai wokim planti gutpela samting long yu. Wanpela tumbuna pikinini long lain bilong yu bai kamap king long ol manmeri bilong mi oltaim oltaim. Dispela wanpela man em i kam long lain bilong Devit na em inap long kamap king oltaim oltaim, em i Mesaia. Mesaia em i man God i makim bilong kisim bek ol manmeri bilong dispela graun long pasin nogut bilong ol.

==== Translation Notes: ====


  * **the prophet Nathan** – It may be more natural in some languages to say, "a prophet named Nathan."
  * **man of war** - That is, "man who fights battles." David killed many, many people in battle against the Israelites' enemies. God wasn't punishing David, but God wanted a man of peace to build the Temple where people would worship him.
  * **this Temple** - That is, "this building for worship" or, "this place of worship."
  * **from sin** - That is, "from the terrible consequences of their sin."
