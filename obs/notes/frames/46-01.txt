===== Saul Arrested the Believers [46-01] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-01.jpg?nolink&}}

Sol em i yanpela man husat i bin lukautim ol saket bilong ol man husat i bin tromoi ston long Stiven. Em i no bilip man olsem na em i bin kilim ol lain husat i bilip long Jisas. Em i save i go long wanwan haus insait long Jerusalem na holim ol manmeri, na kalabusim ol. Hetpris i givim tok orait long Sol long i go long Damaskus na kisim ol Kristen i kam long Jerusalem.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **guarded the robes of the men who killed Stephen** - See **[[:tpi:obs:notes:frames:45-06|[45-06]]]**.
  * **from house to house** - That is, "into many houses."
  * **Damascus** - Damascus is now the capital of the country of Syria. During Saul's time, it was a city belonging to the Roman Empire. Most of the people there were not Jewish, but there were some Jews and also some Christians living there.
