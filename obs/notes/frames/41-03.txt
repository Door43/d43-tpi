===== The Women [41-03] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-03.jpg?nolink&}}

De bihain ol i planim Jisas, em i de Sabat. Na em i tambu tru long olgeta lain Juda long i go long matmat long de Sabat. Olsem na long monintaim long de bihain long de Sabat sampela meri i bin redim sampela smel sanda long kisim i go long matmat. Ol i bin tingting long putim smel sanda long bodi bilong Jisas.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the Jews were not permitted** - The Sabbath day rules did not permit a Jew to walk very far or to do any other kind of work.
  * **burial spices** - This refers to sweet-smelling spices that were placed on a dead body to cover the bad smell. This can also be translated as, "sweet-smelling spices" or, "sweet-smelling oils" or, "sweet-smelling plants."
