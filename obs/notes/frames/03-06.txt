===== The Animals Came [03-06] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg?nolink&}}

God i salim wanpela man na wanpela meri bilong olgeta kain abus na pisin long i go long Noa na i go insait long sip. Olgeta gutpela abus na pisin bilong wokim ofa long God tasol. God i salim kainkain 7-pela man na 7-pela meri animal long i go long Noa na i go insait long sip. Olgeta bai i go insait long sip na stap gut taim bikpela tait wara i kamap na bagarapim olgeta laip long graun. Taim olgeta i go insait long sip pinis, God yet i pasim dua bilong sip.

===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **God sent** - Noah did not need to find the animals. God sent them to him.
  * **used for sacrifices** - For some languages it may be better to say something like, "animals that were acceptable to sacrifice to God." God had decided that people should sacrifice animals to him, but he only permitted them to sacrifice certain kinds of animals.
  * **God himself closed the door** - This emphasizes that it was God who shut the door.
