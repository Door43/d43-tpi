===== Dry Land [03-12] =====

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?nolink&}}

Noa i wet long wanpela moa wik na em i salim pisin balus namba 3 taim gen. Tasol long dispela taim pisin i painim drai graun long malolo olsem na en i no kam bek gen long Noa. Noa i save, planti hap graun i bin drai pinis.

===== Important Terms: =====


  * **[[:tpi:obe:other:noah]]**


===== Translation Notes: =====


  * **waited another week** - You could say, "waited seven more days." The word "waited" shows that Noah was allowing time for the floodwaters to go down before sending out the dove again.
