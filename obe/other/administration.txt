====== administration, administrator ======

===== Facts: =====

Administration references a system of management or governance. An administrator is one who manages or governs.

  * The King of Babylon, Darius, divided his kingdom into 120 states or satraps. Three adminstrators, or "commissioners," were over all the satraps.
  * The prophet Daniel was one of the three.
  * He rose above the other two because of his "extraordinary spirit" and management ability.
