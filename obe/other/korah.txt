====== korah ======

===== Definition: =====

An Israelite leader who participated in a rebellion against Moses during the exodus journey.

  * Korah was a Levite and was jealous of the position and authority given to Moses by God.
  * Korah conspired with Dathan and Abiram to divide Israel's loyalty to their God-given leader Moses.
  * God took the lives of the conspirators by opening up the earth so that it swallowed them alive.

(See also: other)
