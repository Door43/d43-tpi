====== Mishael ======

===== Facts: =====

Mishael is the name of three people in the Old Testament.

  * A Levite cousin of Aaron who was charged along with another to dispose of the bodies of two men who had desecrated the altar.
  * A man named Mishael stood beside Ezra when he publically read the rediscovered law..
  * One of Daniels three friends who was renamed by the Babylonians, Meshach, who endured the fiery furnace with Shadrach and Abednego.
