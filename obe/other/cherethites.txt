====== Cherethites ======

===== Facts: =====

The Cherethites were select soldiers from David's army that demonstrated  absolute loyalty to him.

  * Benaiah, son of Jehoiada, a member of David's administrative corps, was their leader.
  * The Cherethites remained with David when he had to flee Jerusalem because of Absalom's revolt.
