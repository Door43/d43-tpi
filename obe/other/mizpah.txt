====== Mizpah ======

===== Facts: =====

Mizpah is the name of several towns described in the Old Testament. The word means look-out point or watchtower.

  * Mizpah of Gilead (Genesis 31:49) is possibly the site of the covenant in Genesis and the base of operations for Jephthah.
  * The land of Mizpah, referenced in Joshua 11:3, was most likely found North of Palestine.
  * Mizpah of Judah was located in the Shephelah and was assigned to the tribe of Judah.
  * Mizpah of Moab, site unknown, was the place David left his parents inder the protection of the King of Moab when David was being pursued by Saul.
  * Mizpah of Benjamin, located on the border of Judah and Israel, was the most important and significant city by that name. It served as an important military and political center, and probably served as the major fortress on the boundry between the two nations. The city was destoyed in the fall of Judah in 597 B.C.
