====== leopard ======

===== Facts: =====

A large wild cat with brown with black spots which live in the hilly regions of Lebanon.

  * The leopard is used figuratively in Bible prophecy because of it's fierce, swift nature.
  * A leopard was used to symbolically describe the power of one of the beasts in the visions given to Daniel.
