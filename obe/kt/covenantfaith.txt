====== covenant faithfulness ======

===== Definition: =====

This term is used to describe God's unwaivering ability and desire to fulfill His promises that He made with Israel.

  * God made agreements with Israel called covenants where He promised to bless and set them apart. God keeps His promises to His people.
  * Some of God's covenants were conditional, meaning He would fulfill it only if Israel fulfilled their duties to Him. Other covenants were unconditional and God stayed faithful in fulfilling them even though Israel disobeyed.
  * Faithfulness to His covenants is a way God demonstrates His grace to His people.
