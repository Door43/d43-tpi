===== Door43 (tpi) =====

Welcome to Door43, an openly-collaborative platform for creating, translating, and distributing open-licensed discipleship resources in any language.

==== Resources ====

These are the resources currently available for translation and use:

  * **[[:tpi:obs|Open Bible Stories]]** – A visual mini-Bible in any language, in text, audio, and video formats.​
  * [[:tpi:obs:notes|translationNotes]] – Translation and exegetical notes for Open Bible Stories.
  * [[:tpi:lexicon|Open Bible Lexicon]] – A Biblical lexicon, intended for integration with Open Bible Stories and Bible translations.
  * [[:tpi:library|Open Christian Library]] – A growing catalog of open-licensed Biblical resources.
  * [[:tpi:ta|translationAcademy]] – A modular, media-based, self-paced training course for translators and translation facilitators.

Door43 is in active development, as we expand the usefulness of the content by developing ways of making it available in more formats and on more mobile devices. Find out how you can help with **[[:en:dev|Door43 Development]]**.
